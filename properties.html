Sports Properties

Exploring the physical and abstract aspects of sports

Physical Properties (Sports Facilities)

  • Stadiums/Arenas:
    • Capacity
    • Seating arrangements
    • Facilities for spectators (concessions, restrooms, etc.)
  • Fields/Courts:
    • Dimensions
    • Surface type (grass, turf, hardwood, etc.)
    • Lighting

Abstract Properties (Characteristics or Features)

  • Intensity: High-intensity vs. low-intensity sports
  • Team Size: Individual sports vs. team sports
  • Duration: Length of games or events
